Install Now

After you downloaded the app you will not open the app. The iOS display an alert and they require you trust this profile. Please do following this step.

  • In iOS 9.1 and lower, go to Settings - General - Profiles - tap on Infinityworldweb Profile - tap on Trust button
  • In iOS 9.2+, go to: Settings - General - Profiles & Device Management - tap on Infinityworldweb Profile - tap on Trust button
  • In iOS 10+, go to: Settings - General - Device Management - tap on Infinityworldweb Profile - tap on Trust button.
Now you can use the app. Enjoy!
